# 12 — Sales Scripts

| Field  | Value             |
| ------ | ----------------- |
| Status | `ready-for-build` |

All scripts: full name first, tagline when closing, no guarantee language.

---

## 1. Cold call (lender / LO)

**Open:** “Hi [Name], this is [You] with Lending Readiness Partners. We help loan officers keep near-miss borrowers in the conversation with a structured mortgage readiness plan—and partner visibility. Do you have two minutes?”

**Pivot:** “When a borrower isn’t ready yet, what happens to that relationship today?”

**Offer:** “I’d like to send a one-page quick reference and book a 15-minute briefing—no pressure, claim-safe materials your compliance team can review.”

**Close:** “Helping More Borrowers Become Lending Ready. What’s a better time—Tuesday or Thursday morning?”

---

## 2. Voicemail

“Hi [Name], [You] with Lending Readiness Partners. We partner with loan officers on mortgage readiness—advisory plans when the answer is ‘not yet,’ with progress updates for your team. I’ll email a leave-behind. My number is [phone]. Again, Lending Readiness Partners.”

---

## 3. Networking (mixer)

“I’m with Lending Readiness Partners—we help borrowers prepare for the next financing conversation and keep LOs informed. We’re not a lender and we don’t guarantee approvals. If you ever have clients who need a plan instead of ‘call me later,’ I’d love to share our partner kit.”

---

## 4. Trade show booth

**Greeter:** “Are you originating or partnering with loan officers?”  
**If yes:** “Here’s our LO quick reference—scan for the digital kit. Want a two-minute walkthrough of the referral loop?”  
**Hand:** Rack card + QR · collect badge scan for follow-up

---

## 5. Chamber of Commerce introduction (30–45 sec)

“Good morning—I’m [Name] with Lending Readiness Partners. We provide mortgage readiness support for lenders, realtors, and borrowers: education, structured plans, and partner visibility. We don’t underwrite or guarantee approvals. If your members work with homebuyers who need time, we’re a claim-safe partner. Happy to connect afterward.”

---

## 6. Elevator pitch (15 sec)

“Lending Readiness Partners helps more borrowers become lending ready—with advisory plans and partner updates—without guarantee culture.”

---

## 7. Discovery call guide (15–20 min)

| Minute | Goal                                              |
| ------ | ------------------------------------------------- |
| 0–2    | Rapport + confirm role                            |
| 2–5    | Current near-miss handling                        |
| 5–8    | Pain: fallout, status chaos, compliance fear      |
| 8–12   | Show partnership model (Refer→Plan→Update→Return) |
| 12–15  | Boundaries: advisory only; staff-mediated         |
| 15–18  | Kit leave-behind + pilot suggestion               |
| 18–20  | Next step: kickoff or send packet                 |

**Discovery questions**

1. Roughly how many files per month stall on credit/docs?
2. Who owns borrower communication when it’s “not yet”?
3. What would “good updates” look like for your LOs?
4. Any compliance constraints on co-marketing?
5. Ideal pilot size for 90 days?
