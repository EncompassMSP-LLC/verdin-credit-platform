## Welcome

If you are reading this, you care about becoming ready for a home financing conversation. That alone puts you ahead of “wait and hope.”

This guide explains common credit topics in plain language. It does **not** promise loan approval, a specific score, or a closing date. Your loan officer and lender make lending decisions. Lending Readiness Partners (LRP) helps you organize education, tasks, and progress—and keeps your partner informed when you ask us to.

## How to use this guide

1. Skim the chapter map; start where your LO or coach pointed you
2. Complete the reflection questions at chapter ends
3. Track tasks in your borrower portal (if enrolled)
4. Bring questions to your LO—not internet rumor mills

## What “lending ready” means here

Being **lending ready** means you understand your credit picture, you have a prioritized plan, and you are progressing on documentation and habits that matter for a mortgage conversation. It is **not** a guarantee that any lender will approve you.
