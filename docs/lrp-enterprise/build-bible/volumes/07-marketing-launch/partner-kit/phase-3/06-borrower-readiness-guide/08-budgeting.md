# Chapter 8 — Budgeting

Mortgage readiness includes cash-flow readiness. Lenders look at income and obligations; your daily budget decides whether the plan is sustainable.

## Simple monthly map

1. Income (net)
2. Housing (current)
3. Minimum debt payments
4. Essentials (food, transport, utilities, insurance)
5. Savings / reserves
6. Discretionary

## Habits

- Automate minimums to protect payment history
- Build a small reserve before stretching for a purchase
- Track subscriptions and “leak” spending for 30 days

Bring a honest budget to coaching—not a perfect one.
