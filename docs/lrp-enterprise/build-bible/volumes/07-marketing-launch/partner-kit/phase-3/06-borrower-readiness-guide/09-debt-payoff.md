# Chapter 9 — Debt payoff strategy

There is no single correct method for everyone. Common approaches include avalanche (highest interest first) and snowball (smallest balance first for momentum). Your coach may blend methods with utilization and mortgage timing in mind.

## Principles

- Protect on-time payments above all
- Align extra payments with your readiness plan
- Don’t open new debt to “fix” old debt without advice
- Document settlements carefully

**We do not guarantee** that any payoff sequence produces a specific score or approval.
