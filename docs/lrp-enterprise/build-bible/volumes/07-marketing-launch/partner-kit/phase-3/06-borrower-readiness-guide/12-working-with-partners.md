# Chapter 12 — Working with your loan officer & LRP

## Who does what

| Person / team | Role                                                       |
| ------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------- |
| You           | Tasks, honesty, documents, habits                          |
| Loan officer  | Lending path, product advice, underwriting with lender     |
| Realtor       | Home search expectations (if applicable)                   |
| LRP           | Education, readiness plan, updates, staff-mediated support |

## Communication tips

- One source of truth for status (portal / LO)
- Ask “what do you need from me this week?”
- Don’t hide new debts or job changes
- Celebrate progress without inventing score miracles
