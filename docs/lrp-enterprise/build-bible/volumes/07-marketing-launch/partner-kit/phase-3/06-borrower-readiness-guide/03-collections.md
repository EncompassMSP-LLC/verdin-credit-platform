# Chapter 3 — Collections

A collection means a debt was placed with a collection company or is reported that way. Collections often need a careful plan—not panic.

## What to do first

1. Confirm the debt is yours and the balance/status is accurate
2. Gather statements, letters, and payment records
3. Ask your readiness coach / LO before contacting collectors if you are in a structured program
4. Understand that paying or disputing can have different effects depending on the situation

## Claim-safe language

We help you organize documentation and, where appropriate, staff-mediated dispute workflows. We do **not** promise deletion or a specific score change.

## Reflection

List each collection: creditor, approximate balance, last activity, documents you have.
