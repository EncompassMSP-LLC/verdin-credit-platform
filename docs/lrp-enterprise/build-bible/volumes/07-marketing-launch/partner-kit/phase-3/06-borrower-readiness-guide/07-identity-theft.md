# Chapter 7 — Identity theft

If accounts, addresses, or inquiries appear that are not yours, treat it seriously and calmly.

## Immediate steps (general education)

1. Document what looks wrong (screenshots/PDFs)
2. Contact your readiness coach and loan officer
3. Use official bureau fraud / freeze processes as advised
4. File reports with appropriate agencies when warranted
5. Avoid posting full account numbers or SSN in email/chat

LRP may help organize documentation and staff-mediated workflows. We do not make legal conclusions in marketing materials. Complex identity cases may require counsel.
