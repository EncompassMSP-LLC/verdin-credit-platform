# Chapter 11 — Maintaining credit after approval

If you receive a loan approval or clear-to-close path from your lender, protect the work you did.

## Common cautions (general education)

- Avoid new credit applications before closing unless your LO says otherwise
- Keep paying every account on time
- Large unexplained deposits/withdrawals can raise questions—ask your LO
- Don’t change jobs mid-process without talking to your LO
- Keep your phone on for conditions and document requests

Approval language belongs to your lender—not to LRP marketing.
