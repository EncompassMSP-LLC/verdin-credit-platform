# Chapter 5 — Utilization

Utilization is roughly how much of your available revolving credit you are using. High utilization is a common reason “not yet” shows up even when payments are on time.

## Practical habits

- Know balances and limits on each revolving account
- Prioritize high-utilization cards in your payment plan
- Avoid closing cards solely for optics without coaching—length of history can matter
- Time large payments with statement dates when advised

## What we won’t say

We will not promise “X points if you pay Y.” We will help you build a payment priority list.
