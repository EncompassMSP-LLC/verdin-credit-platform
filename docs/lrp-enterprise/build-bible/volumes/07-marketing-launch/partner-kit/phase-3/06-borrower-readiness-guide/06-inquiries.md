# Chapter 6 — Inquiries

An inquiry is a record that someone pulled your credit. Mortgage shopping within a short window is often treated differently than scattering applications across many product types—but rules vary by model and product.

## Habits while preparing

- Ask before applying for new cards, autos, or furniture financing
- Tell retailers “no thanks” to instant credit offers at checkout
- Coordinate rate shopping with your LO’s guidance
- Don’t panic over a single inquiry—context matters

If you see inquiries you don’t recognize, tell your coach promptly (see Identity theft chapter).
