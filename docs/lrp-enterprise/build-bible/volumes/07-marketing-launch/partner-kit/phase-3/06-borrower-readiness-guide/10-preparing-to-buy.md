# Chapter 10 — Preparing to buy a home

Buying readiness is more than credit. Work with your LO on:

| Area            | Examples                                       |
| --------------- | ---------------------------------------------- |
| Income          | Paystubs, W-2s, tax returns, self-emp docs     |
| Assets          | Statements, gift letter paths                  |
| Employment      | Stability questions                            |
| Housing history | Landlord contacts / mortgage history           |
| Identity        | Government ID, SSN verification path           |
| Property goals  | Price range, location, timeline (aspirational) |

## Mindset

“Not yet” is information—not a verdict on your worth. A clear plan beats disappearing for six months.
