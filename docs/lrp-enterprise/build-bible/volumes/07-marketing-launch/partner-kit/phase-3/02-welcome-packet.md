## 2. Company overview

**Headline:** Who we are

Lending Readiness Partners is a Mortgage Readiness Solutions provider. We help credit operators, lenders, loan officers, and realtors give near-miss borrowers a dignified, structured path toward the next financing conversation.

**What we offer partners**

- Advisory Lending Readiness Score™ and progress framing
- Staff-mediated dispute and documentation workflows (where applicable)
- Partner visibility into referral status and readiness progress
- Borrower education and task completion support
- Claim-safe communication your compliance team can review

**What we are not**

- A creditor or lender
- An underwriting engine
- A guarantee of approval, rates, or funding
- An unsupervised bureau-filing service

**Category line:** AI-powered Borrower Readiness Platform — education, strategy, and progress for the next financing opportunity.

## 4. Why partner with us

**For loan officers and lenders**

- Retain relationships when a file is early
- Replace tribal status updates with a shared process
- Offer a specialist path without becoming a credit-repair shop
- Keep messaging claim-safe for brand and compliance

**For realtors**

- Keep buyers engaged when financing needs more time
- Set expectations with a shared readiness vocabulary
- Prefer coordinated paths with preferred lenders

**For borrowers (partner-facing framing)**

- Know where they stand and what to do next
- Learn credit concepts that matter for mortgages
- Progress with tasks—not miracle promises

**Differentiation:** structured analysis, advisory readiness, partner dashboards, and staff-mediated operations—comparable to an enterprise service provider, not a flyer mill.

## 6. What to expect

### First 14 days

| Day  | Milestone                                                 |
| ---- | --------------------------------------------------------- |
| 0    | Partnership record active; seats / contacts confirmed     |
| 1–3  | Kickoff call; digest cadence agreed; claim library shared |
| 3–7  | Portal / login cards issued; first referral dry-run       |
| 7–14 | First live referrals accepted; update template locked     |

### Ongoing cadence

| Cadence      | What partners receive                                 |
| ------------ | ----------------------------------------------------- |
| Per referral | Accept / decline acknowledgment                       |
| Weekly       | Progress digest (pipeline-level; PII minimized)       |
| As needed    | Escalations when borrower stalls or docs missing      |
| Quarterly    | Partnership review (volume, cycle time, messaging QA) |

### Referral → return loop

1. LO / realtor refers borrower (form or portal)
2. LRP intake reviews completeness
3. Borrower onboards to education + tasks
4. Staff-mediated work proceeds where required
5. Partner sees status; borrower resumes lending conversation when prepared

### Service boundaries

| We can                                         | We cannot                                     |
| ---------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------- |
| Educate and organize readiness work            | Guarantee scores, approvals, or closing dates |
| Provide advisory readiness signals             | Replace underwriting or AUS decisions         |
| Support staff-reviewed dispute workflows       | File unsupervised with bureaus                |
| Share scoped progress with authorized partners | Cross-tenant marketplace of borrower files    |

## 9. Claim & compliance posture (1-pager)

**Headline:** How we communicate

Partners and LRP agree that public and borrower-facing materials will follow the LRP claim library:

- Advisory readiness only—never promised approval or funding
- No fabricated FICO before/after claims
- No unsupervised bureau filing representations
- Staff-mediated language for dispute support

**Partner marketing review:** Any co-branded flyer, email, or social post using LRP’s name or score must be reviewed before publish.

**Privacy:** Referral data is used to deliver readiness services and partner updates. We do not sell borrower lists. See Privacy Notice in the Referral Packet.
