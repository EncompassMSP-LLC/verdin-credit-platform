# 13 — Objection Handling Guide

| Field  | Value             |
| ------ | ----------------- |
| Status | `ready-for-build` |

Use with LO quick reference and sales scripts. Stay calm, precise, claim-safe.

---

## “How long will this take?”

**Respond:** “It depends on the file—documentation gaps can move faster than multi-issue credit work. We prioritize tasks and keep you updated on a set cadence. We don’t guarantee a calendar date.”

**Bridge:** “What timeline is your borrower hoping for, so we can be honest about fit?”

---

## “How much does it cost?”

**Respond:** “Partnership and consumer engagement terms are reviewed in the briefing so we match scope—pilot vs ongoing. We’re transparent; we don’t sell miracle packages.”

**Bridge:** “I can send the welcome packet overview and walk pricing options on a short call.”

---

## “Can you guarantee results?”

**Respond:** “No—and you should be cautious of anyone who does. We don’t guarantee score changes, approvals, or funding. We organize education, tasks, and staff-mediated support, and we keep partners informed.”

**Bridge:** “Our materials are claim-library locked for that reason.”

---

## “Why shouldn’t I use another company?”

**Respond:** “You should evaluate fit. What we emphasize is partner visibility, advisory readiness—not underwriting cosplay—and compliance-minded communication. Many shops optimize for consumer hype; we optimize for lender trust.”

**Bridge:** “Compare our leave-behind and portal concept side by side with anyone you’re considering.”

---

## “Will this delay my closing?”

**Respond:** “If a borrower isn’t ready, forcing an application can create worse outcomes. Our job is preparation. When they’re progressing, we help them return to you—we don’t sit on a clear-to-close file to ‘run out a package.’”

**Bridge:** “For files already in underwriting with your lender, coordinate with your processor; we’re not a substitute.”
