## A. Tri-fold brochure (8.5" × 11" flat → 3 panels)

Panel order when fully open (inside left → center → right), then outside.

### Outside — Front panel (cover)

**Brand:** Lending Readiness Partners  
**Program:** Mortgage Readiness Program  
**Tagline:** Helping More Borrowers Become Lending Ready.  
**Support line:** Education, strategy, and progress for the next financing opportunity.  
**Visual:** Full-bleed atmospheric homeownership image (porch light / keys / plans)—not credit-repair clichés.  
**QR:** `/lenders` · caption “Partner briefing”

### Outside — Back panel (center when folded)

**Contact**

```
Lending Readiness Partners
Partnerships
[phone] · [email]
[website]
```

**QR:** `/contact?intent=lender`  
**Footer disclaimer**

### Outside — Inside flap (right when folded = first reveal)

**Headline:** When “not yet” needs a plan  
**Body:** Motivated borrowers stall when credit and documentation gaps are unclear. Lending Readiness Partners helps organize the path forward—while keeping loan officers and realtors informed.

### Inside — Left panel: Why partner

**Headline:** Why partners choose LRP

- Priority handling for mortgage-intent referrals
- Personalized credit and documentation plans
- Regular progress updates for partners
- Mortgage-focused readiness strategy (advisory—not underwriting)
- Responsive partner communication
- Secure portals for borrowers and partners

### Inside — Center panel: How it works

**Headline:** A clear process

1. Borrower applies or is referred
2. Credit or documentation gaps identified
3. Referral sent to Lending Readiness Partners
4. Education and readiness plan begins
5. Progress updates to the partner
6. Advisory Lending Ready signal when appropriate
7. Borrower returns to the LO for the next financing conversation

**Callout:** We support readiness. Your team underwrites.

### Inside — Right panel: What partners get

**Headline:** Visibility without confusion

- Referral status you can explain
- Borrower tasks and education in a portal
- Staff-mediated dispute support where required
- Claim-safe language for your brand

**CTA:** Schedule a briefing · `/lenders`

## E. Copy blocks (canonical — reuse across variants)

**Cover program title:** Mortgage Readiness Program

**Cover support:** Helping more families move toward homeownership readiness—with a plan you can explain.

**Never print:**

- Guaranteed approval / funding
- Fabricated FICO before/after
- “We’ll get you a mortgage”
- Unsupervised filing claims

**Always print:** short advisory disclaimer on every exterior panel that carries a CTA or score mention.
