## 2. Authorization (partner status sharing)

**Title:** Authorization to Share Readiness Progress with My Loan Officer / Referral Partner

**Body (draft):**  
I authorize Lending Readiness Partners to share limited progress and status information related to my mortgage readiness program with the loan officer and/or referral partner named below, for the purpose of coordinating my financing conversation. I understand this does not authorize underwriting decisions by LRP and does not guarantee loan approval.

**Signature / date / partner named**

## 3. Intake checklist (LRP)

- [ ] Referral complete
- [ ] Consents present or requested
- [ ] Conflict / duplicate check
- [ ] Product intent noted
- [ ] Accept / needs-info / decline sent
- [ ] Borrower portal invite
- [ ] Partner acknowledgment

---

## 4. Document checklist (borrower)

- [ ] Government-issued ID
- [ ] Proof of income path (as requested)
- [ ] Housing history notes
- [ ] Collection / dispute letters (if any)
- [ ] Creditor login access (borrower-owned)
- [ ] Signed consents

## 5. Privacy notice (summary page)

We collect information to deliver readiness services and partner updates. We do not sell borrower lists. Access is role-based and auditable. For the full privacy policy, see [website privacy URL]. Cross-tenant marketplace sharing is not offered.
