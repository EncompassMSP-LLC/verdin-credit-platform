# 08 — Marketing Flyers (5 creatives)

| Field  | Value              |
| ------ | ------------------ |
| Status | `ready-for-build`  |
| Size   | 8.5" × 11" (or A4) |
| Finish | Matte              |

Each flyer: one headline, one short support, one CTA group, QR, short disclaimer. No guarantee claims. No fabricated FICO.

---

## Flyer 1 — “Didn’t Qualify?”

**Headline:** Didn’t Qualify?  
**Support:** “Not yet” can still have a clear plan—while your loan officer stays informed.  
**Bullets:** Education · Structured tasks · Partner updates  
**CTA:** Talk to your LO · `/contact?intent=borrower`  
**Brand:** Lending Readiness Partners · Tagline  
**Disclaimer:** Advisory readiness only—not a loan approval.

---

## Flyer 2 — “Mortgage Ready in 90 Days”

**Headline:** Building Toward Mortgage Ready  
**Support:** Many files need weeks or months—not miracles. Ask about a structured 90-day readiness sprint with prioritized tasks.  
**Note for design:** Do **not** imply guaranteed readiness in 90 days. Subhead must say “a structured focus period—not a promise.”  
**CTA:** Ask your loan officer about Lending Readiness Partners  
**Disclaimer:** required

---

## Flyer 3 — “Credit Holding You Back?”

**Headline:** Credit Holding You Back?  
**Support:** Let’s organize the work: utilization, collections, documentation, and next steps—with dignity.  
**CTA:** Scan to learn more · partner kit / contact  
**Visual:** Calm homeownership atmosphere

---

## Flyer 4 — “Preparing for Homeownership”

**Headline:** Preparing for Homeownership  
**Support:** Credit education, budgeting habits, and document readiness—so your next financing conversation is clearer.  
**CTA:** Borrower guide / portal invite via LO

## Flyer 5 — “Partner With Us”

**Headline:** Partner With Us  
**Support:** Mortgage Readiness Partnership for lenders and realtors—advisory progress visibility, staff-mediated process, claim-safe marketing.  
**CTA:** `/lenders` · `/realtors` · Book a briefing  
**Audience:** B2B (LO desks, realtor offices)
