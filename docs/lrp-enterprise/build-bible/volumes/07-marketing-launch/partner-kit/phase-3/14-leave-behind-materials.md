# 14 — Leave-Behind Materials

| Field  | Value             |
| ------ | ----------------- |
| Status | `ready-for-build` |

Companion pieces for desks, counters, and door-to-door partner drops. Palette and type from brand style guide.

---

## Desk cards (3.5" × 2" or 4" × 3")

**Front:** Wordmark · “Mortgage Readiness Partnership” · tagline  
**Back:** QR to `/resources/partner-kit` · phone · short disclaimer

---

## Referral pads (50-sheet)

Header: Lending Readiness Partners — Referral Note  
Fields: Borrower name, phone, product intent, known issues, LO name, date  
Footer: “Submit via secure form: /resources/partner-kit/referral” · disclaimer  
Tear-off branding strip with QR

---

## Counter displays (acrylic insert)

Insert size per acrylic (often 4×9 or 5×7). Use rack card art. Headline: “Ask about mortgage readiness support.”

---

## Door hangers (4" × 9" with thumb hole)

**Audience:** Realtor / LO offices (with permission)  
**Front:** “When buyers need more time”  
**Back:** Process strip + QR to `/realtors` or `/lenders`  
**Do not** place on residential doors without local solicitation rules review.

---

## Table tents (two-sided)

**Side A:** Partner With Us (B2B)  
**Side B:** Didn’t Qualify? Ask your LO (borrower-facing)  
Short disclaimer both sides.
