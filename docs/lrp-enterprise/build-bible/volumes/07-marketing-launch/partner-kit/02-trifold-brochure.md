## Inside — Why partner with us?

- Priority handling for mortgage-intent referrals
- Personalized credit and documentation plans
- Regular progress updates for partners
- Mortgage-focused readiness strategy (advisory—not underwriting)
- Responsive partner communication
- Secure portals for borrowers and partners

## Inside — Process

1. Borrower applies / is referred
2. Credit or documentation gaps identified
3. Referral sent to Lending Readiness Partners
4. Credit improvement and education plan
5. Progress updates to partner
6. Lending Ready signal (advisory band)
7. Borrower returns to LO for the next financing conversation
