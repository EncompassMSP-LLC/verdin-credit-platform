# Realtor co-channel kit

Separate messaging for real estate professionals. Web: `/realtors`.

## Core messages

- Keep buyers engaged while they improve credit and documentation readiness
- Reduce lost sales due to silent financing stalls
- Stay updated on buyer progress with shared readiness language
- Receive educational resources to share with clients

## Cover line

**Help buyers stay in the conversation while they become lending ready.**

## Flyer bullets

- Shared readiness stages agents can explain ethically
- Updates that protect dignity—no shame narratives
- Preferred-partner path through lenders and operators
- Expectation-setting scripts from the Partner Kit
