# Email drips — lenders, realtors, borrowers

All footers: tagline + short advisory disclaimer. No approval/funding guarantees.

## A. Lender drip (5) — expand Phase 1 `07`

1. Helping you prepare more borrowers for financing conversations
2. What happens when a borrower isn’t ready yet?
3. Introducing our Mortgage Readiness Partnership
4. Keep more clients from walking away
5. Let’s help more families become lending ready

CTA each: `/contact?intent=lender` or `/lenders`.

## B. Realtor drip (5)

1. Subject: Keep buyers engaged when financing needs more time  
   Body: Shared readiness stages; dignity-first updates. CTA `/realtors`.
2. Subject: Don’t lose the conversation  
   Body: “Not yet” with a plan; preferred lender path.
3. Subject: Expectation-setting scripts your agents can defend  
   Body: Kit resources; no miracle claims.
4. Subject: How updates work with your preferred lender  
   Body: Cadence + what agents can say.
5. Subject: Partner with readiness—not hype  
   Body: Close + briefing CTA `/contact?intent=realtor`.

## C. Borrower drip (5) — post-enrollment tone

1. Subject: Welcome—here’s how readiness works  
   Body: Portal intro; advisory score explanation.
2. Subject: Your next three actions  
   Body: Tasks only; encouragement without promises.
3. Subject: Why documentation matters  
   Body: Education pillar.
4. Subject: How we keep your loan officer informed  
   Body: Partner visibility; privacy.
5. Subject: You’re building toward lending ready  
   Body: Progress framing; disclaimer; support CTA.
