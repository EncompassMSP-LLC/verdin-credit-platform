# Co-branded partner portal — onboarding concept

Concept for lender/realtor onboarding beyond current interim staff JWT. Complements `/lender` realm.

## Goals

- First-run checklist for new partnership admins
- Co-brand slot (partner logo + “Powered by Lending Readiness Partners”)
- Link to welcome packet + kit hub
- First referral walkthrough
- Claim-library acknowledgment

## Screens (concept)

1. **Welcome** — tagline, partnership name, disclaimer
2. **Confirm contacts** — LO list, digest email
3. **Brand** — optional logo upload (compliance review)
4. **Workspace tour** — pipeline, referrals, borrower tracking
5. **First referral** — deep link to create/list referrals
6. **Done** — CTA to `/resources/partner-kit` and support

## Implementation notes

- Phase 2 **docs + hub page** only; full UX may ship when partner JWT lands (Vol 24 follow-up).
- Hub: `/resources/partner-kit/phase-2` section “Portal onboarding”.
- Do not imply white-label independence from LRP claim rules.
