# LinkedIn campaign — themes & captions

## Message pillars

1. Mortgage partnership (institutional tone)
2. Helping more borrowers become lending ready
3. Reduce credit-related fallout / keep relationships
4. Lending Ready as an advisory signal
5. Your readiness partner—not a creditor

## Sample captions

**Partnership**  
Serious lenders need readiness partners who respect underwriting. We provide advisory progress and partner visibility—never approval theater.

**Qualify language (safe)**  
We help borrowers organize credit and documentation work so they’re better prepared for the next financing conversation.

**Close more loans (softened)**  
When “not yet” has a plan, fewer relationships walk away. See how our Mortgage Readiness Partnership works.

**Mortgage Ready**  
“Lending Ready” at LRP is an advisory band for partners and borrowers—not a loan decision.

**Credit partner**  
You originate. We help clients become lending ready—with updates you can explain.

## Creative notes

- Prefer navy/gold institutional layouts
- Avoid red “DENIED” shock graphics and miracle score claims
- Always include tagline or short disclaimer on image text when space allows
